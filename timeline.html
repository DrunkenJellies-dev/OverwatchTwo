Over 30 Years Ago

The Omnica Crisis

Tensions surged between Omnica Corporation and its malfunctioning omniums, triggering the Omnic Crisis. In response, the UN formed Overwatch, led by Gabriel Reyes and Jack Morrison, to combat the hostile omnics.

Overwatch fought global battles, scoring victories against the omnics. But internal conflicts emerged, signaling rifts within the organization that would later lead to its fracture.

Omnic Crists Resolution

After the Omnic Crisis, Overwatch emerged as a global symbol of unity. But internal tensions, notably between Jack Morrison (Soldier: 76) and Gabriel Reyes (Reaper), escalated. Their clash at Overwatch's HQ, the "Swiss Headquarters Uprising," led to their apparent demise and the organization's dissolution by the UN.

With Overwatch disbanded, the world plunged into chaos. Former heroes turned outlaws, and the era of uncertainty paved the way for a new timeline. Remnants of Overwatch's legacy and emerging threats now shape the world's narrative.

Overwatch Reformation

The recall initiated by Winston garnered mixed responses. Some answered the call eagerly, rejoining the fight for justice. However, others remained elusive or were embroiled in their own pursuits, reluctant to embrace Overwatch's revival due to lingering distrust or personal reasons.

During this period, the resurgence of Overwatch's communication sparked hope for a new era of heroism, but challenges persisted as the world hesitated to fully embrace the return of the legendary organization.

Overwatch Reformation Continued

Winston's call rallied former Overwatch agents like Reinhardt, Tracer, and Mei, rekindling the team's purpose. But not everyone returned—doubts, personal aims, and global tensions hindered full reunification. Some hesitated, pursuing personal quests or navigating new conflicts.

The world's mixed response to Overwatch's return echoed a fragile hope amid distrust and uncertainty. For the reformed Overwatch, proving its relevance amidst evolving threats and shifting alliances became a pressing challenge.

The Fall of Overwatch

The fall of Overwatch resulted from internal conflicts, notably between Reyes and Morrison, escalating into the "Swiss Headquarters Uprising." This catastrophic event led to the disbandment of Overwatch by the UN through the Petras Act. Former agents scattered as the once-revered organization became outlawed, leaving a void in global security.

The Venice Incident

The Venice Incident in Overwatch involved a Talon assault on a critical world leaders' meeting in Venice. Talon aimed to disrupt global peace talks, but former Overwatch agents intervened, preventing a catastrophe and protecting the leaders from chaos orchestrated by Reaper, Widowmaker, and Sombra.

7 Years Ago

The Null Sector Uprising

The Null Sector Uprising in Overwatch lore saw extremist Omnic group Null Sector staging a major uprising in London. Former Overwatch heroes, led by Tracer, Reinhardt, and Mercy, intervened, fighting alongside local forces to reclaim the city and prevent a humanitarian crisis.

The Second Omnic Uprising

In Overwatch lore, there hasn't been a specifically termed "Second Omnic Crisis." However, there have been instances of omnic-related conflicts, notably the Null Sector Uprising in London. Former Overwatch heroes intervened to reclaim the city from the extremist omnic group, Null Sector.

5 Years Ago
Present Day

The Resurrection of Overwatch

Winston reactivated Overwatch's communication network, issuing a call to former agents to reunite secretly and restore the team's mission of safeguarding the world.

This revival attempt faced challenges, with varied responses from former members. Some heroes, like Tracer and Mei, enthusiastically answered the call, recommitting themselves to Overwatch's cause. However, not all former agents readily rejoined, citing concerns or personal reasons for their reluctance.
